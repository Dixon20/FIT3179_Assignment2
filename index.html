Treatment and prevention for HIV

ART is a mixture of antiviral drugs that are used to treat people with HIV, and it was first introduced in 1980s. Few decades ago, surviving more than 10 years with HIV was considered a miracle. Now, thanks to ART, HIV is no longer a death sentence and people with HIV can live to old age like people without HIV. ART works by surpressing amount of HIV virus in the body (Viral load) until it is Undetectable. Individuals with Undetectable viral load can not infect other people through sex, and also towards their unborn child if they are pregnant mother. That is why beside HIV treatment, it is also effective tool in reducing mother-child-transmission rate. Good news is that most of the area in the world already has adequate ART coverage except Middle East
